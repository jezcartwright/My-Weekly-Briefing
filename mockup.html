The decision

One change only here: tab strip background is now the active category colour at full saturation. Everything else (masthead, topic cards, accent bar, number colour) is exactly as previously signed off.

Variant A (top row): active tab is the same colour as the ribbon, marked by a white outline. Tab visually merges with the ribbon — feels like one anchored colour band.

Variant B (bottom row): active tab is ~20% darker than the ribbon. Tab still reads as a distinct shape, just deeper in the same colour family.

Tell me which. Or "neither, simpler — just use the bold ribbon, no special active-tab treatment, the inactive white pills do the work" — also a valid option.
