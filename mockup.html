PDF view — widen sections to match live-site feel

The PDF view uses 40 px horizontal padding on its category-title, category-body and header blocks. The live site uses 16 px (the .sa scroll-area padding). On phone that takes ~48 px off the effective topic-card width inside the PDF, which is why the sections feel noticeably narrower than the same topics on the live site.

SelectorCurrent paddingProposed padding
.pdf-header30px 40px 26px30px 16px 26px
.pdf-cat-title16px 40px16px 16px
.pdf-cat-body24px 40px 28px24px 16px 28px

Internal topic-card padding (.pdf-topic 20px 24px 18px) left untouched — that's the card's own breathing room, not a width-restrictor. The dashed orange guides on the CURRENT frames mark the target inner edge (where the PROPOSED layout will sit).

PROPOSED — not yet live. CURRENT (top, with guides) vs PROPOSED (bottom) per platform.
