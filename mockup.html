Working mockup: text-size direct picker

Tap any of the three Aa buttons below. Watch the topic card text scale live. Menu stays open so you can audition sizes. Tap the hamburger to close.

The clever bit: three buttons rendering Aa at their actual sizes replaces the single cycling button. One tap to target instead of two cycles to reach xlarge. You SEE the sizes before tapping, no guessing the cycle order. Menu stays open so you can compare — closes only when you tap the hamburger or elsewhere outside.
