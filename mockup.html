How to read this

Variant 1 is what's currently shipped — the text block occupies the right column of the masthead and centres in the middle of that space.

Variant 2 is what I think you mean: the text block is its own self-sized unit, sits flush left next to the π, and inside that unit the three lines share a centre axis.

Variant 3 is an alternative reading: the block sits flush left, AND inside the block all three lines align flush-left to a shared left edge.

Variant 2 keeps the typographic feel of "the eyebrow words are deliberately different widths and balanced around a centre". Variant 3 reads more like a flag/letterhead style.
