How it scales

Content stays in a max-1100px column on all screens. Orange band always edge-to-edge (no broken-up colour), but the cluster stays at editorial density. Avoids the "skinny banner with empty middle" problem on wide displays.

Type sizes scale up at the iPad-and-above breakpoint. Eyebrow 11→13px, wordmark 24→30px, π logo 56→72px.

Two interactive elements only. Date+avatar pill = account/identity. Hamburger = navigation menu (PDF, Notes, Liked, Library, Font-size). Clean separation by function.

Email template inheritance. The Monday email banner will adopt the same π+eyebrow+wordmark layout but without the interactive pill/hamburger — they have no meaning in email. Keeps brand consistency between web and inbox.
