The 50-Year Career Problem
Midcareer professionals running 50-year careers on systems designed for 30-year working lives, and burnout is the first symptom.

The final spec, applied uniformly

Masthead

  • Date top-left (plain text, white, no pill)
  • Hamburger then Avatar, top-right (avatar at the absolute right edge)
  • π logo centred, 36px (iPhone) up to 48px (larger screens)
  • PERFORMANCE / INTELLIGENCE eyebrow, weight 700, centred, +10% size from earlier mocks, INTELLIGENCE noticeably wider than PERFORMANCE via per-word letter-spacing
  • "Weekly Briefing" wordmark, IBM Plex Serif weight 500 (25% lighter than the earlier 700), upright (not italic), centred

Ribbon under tabs

  • Defaults to orange (matches masthead) — initial load is one continuous orange band
  • Recolours to active category on tab change, with a 350ms transition
  • Active tab: matches ribbon colour, white outline
  • Inactive tabs: white pill with category-coloured text and border

Content area

  • Topic cards, accent bar, "01" number all stay orange — unchanged from current site
